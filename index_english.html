Biography

João Marcos Simon Paulini (born in 2012 in Évora, Portugal) is a violinist, writer, and online French and violin teacher with a prominent role in the cultural scene of Brasília. Co-founder of the KOPA Orchestra (Karla Oliveto Paulinyi Orchestra), he develops an intense pedagogical and artistic activity focused on training young musicians, regularly performing in educational concerts and cultural events. The KOPA Orchestra’s main goal is to promote chamber music and symphonic repertoire adapted to small groups.
Besides his work as an instrumentalist, João Marcos also stands out as a writer, with publications on the Clube de Autores. His literary works include the books:
The Long Journey Around the World – Volume 1
Le Long Voyage Autour du Monde – Tome 1 (French edition)

In the digital environment, João Marcos is the creator of the channel João Marcos - Violin on YouTube, where he shares videos of performances and presentations of his violin concerts. His content is aimed both at beginners and more advanced musicians seeking to improve their technique. João also collaborates with his father, Zoltan Paulinyi, a renowned violinist, composer, and founder of various musical groups and educational projects in Brazil. Together, they participate in activities of the Schola Cantorum of Brasília and performances with Duo SPES, promoting classical music in different contexts, with emphasis on training, innovation, and accessibility. Besides his artistic and pedagogical activities, João Marcos is also a talented web developer, creating modern, functional, and optimized websites with expertise in languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. He combines his artistic sensibility with technology, delivering projects that unite aesthetics, functionality, and creativity.
